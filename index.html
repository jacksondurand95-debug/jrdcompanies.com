About John des Rosiers

With over 25 years of experience in the restaurant industry, John des Rosiers brings unparalleled expertise in culinary arts, restaurant concept development, and hospitality ventures. Based in Lake Bluff, Illinois, John has designed and built numerous food and beverage locations, from fine dining establishments to casual concepts.

His approach combines international culinary education with a focus on both quality and high-volume production, ensuring each venture delivers exceptional experiences.
